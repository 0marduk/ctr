Instructions

Places in the Stadium

The Stadium has several different areas. To the right of the Chat area is a set of links to the different places in the Stadium.

The links look like these:
  • The Stadium Entrance link takes you to the entry point outside of the Stadium.
  • The Spectator Stands takes you inside the Stadium to watch the game.
  • The Control Booth link takes you to the controls for the Stadium scoreboard.
  • The Locker Room links take you to the locker rooms, where you can gain entrance to the field.

The Stadium is usally in Free Play mode, which means anyone can acces all areas of the Stadium, and everyone is welcome to use the Stadium.

The Stadium is also used for Official Games, with pre-registered players on each team. During Official Games, you will only receive the links for areas you are authorized to access. All other areas are off limits.

For eample, during an official game, only Referees will get the Control Booth link, and only Players will receive a link to their team's locker room.

Applause and Boo Controls

To the right of the Chat area you will also see two hand icons.

Click the 'Thumbs Up' icon to cheer.

Click the 'Thumbs Down' icon to boo.

When you cheer or boo, the other people in the Stadium will hear you too.

Playing V-Soccer

Playing V-Soccer is simple, but may require some practice just like any game. Here are some basics to get you started. Be sure to also read the Game Rules following the instructions.

You must be in 3D mode to play V-Soccer. To be in 3D you need Blaxxun CCPro

Go to the V-Soccer Field

  • Go to a Locker Room by clicking a 'Locker Room' link in the right frame.
  • If there is no game today there is one for each team. During Official Games, a Locker Room link will only appear for registered Players.
  • In the Locker Room, click on the door marked 'To Field' to enter the playing field.
  • Your avatar will be automatically changed to a V-Soccer Avatar

Get the Ball

  • Run up to the ball, when you get close enough to pick it up -- you will, unless some one gets it first...
  • You cannot throw the ball twice in a row. Another player must throw the ball before you will be able to pick it up again.
  • Once you have the ball you will see the throw control on your screen. It looks like a red 'lollipop':


    • - Throw Control

Throw the Ball

Click on the red sphere portion of the throw control and drag it downwards.
The farther you drag it down, the farther you will throw the ball. Drag it down less, and you will throw the ball a shorter distance. It works like a pinball plunger.
When you release the mouse button, the ball is thrown.

Note! While you have the ball, you must stay in one place and throw it. You can turn, but not run. If you run with the ball, it is called 'traveling' and you receive a penalty. If you receive a penalty, you are removed to the sidelines and held there for 25 seconds.

Game Rules

  • Each team can have up to 4 players.
  • Players may not move while in posession of the ball. Navigation is disabled until the ball is thrown. If you defeat the disable -- you will receive a penalty.
  • Players may not throw the ball twice in a row. Throw is disabled until another player handles the ball.
  • Any goals scored by a player while traveling will be removed from the score.
  • Additional penaltys/awards for unfair game play may be set by the referees.

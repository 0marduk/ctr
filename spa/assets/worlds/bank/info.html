Cybertown Bank - Information


 

 

How to use the Bank

Transferring CityCash from your account to another has never been easier.  Just follow these step by step instructions!

Step 1: Open the CityCash Transfer Console

Click the CityCash Transfer button under the Jumpgate, or click on one of the 3 CC Transfer Machines in the 3D Bank.

  (Click this button, or click one of the machines below:)

Step 2: Enter Transfer Information

Next, enter the Cybertown Nickname to which you want to transfer funds.  Then, type in the amount of CityCash you wish to transfer.

Finally, click TRANSFER, or if you wish to cancel the transaction, click CANCEL.

Step 3: Confirm Transfer Information

If the transfer information you entered appears to be correct, click CONFIRM.  If you would like to make a change, click BACK.

Step 4: Transaction Complete

Now that your transaction has completed, you may perform another transaction by clicking NEW, or you may quit by clicking CLOSE.

Rules and Regulations

Your use of the Cybertown Bank is subject to the rules of the Cybertown Constitution and Legal Notices. Any abuse of the facilities will result in appropriate action being taken under the UBP. We operate a policy of honesty with the bank, please treat others as you yourself would like to be treated.

Technical Support

If you experience problems with the bank, you can contact a Cybertown Community Technician who will be happy to assist you. You can contact them by using the Problem Report form which can be found here. Please note that all transactions are final and ireversible, however in the event of a security breach, you will be re-imbursed.
